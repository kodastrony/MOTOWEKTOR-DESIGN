## 5. Dealer / Partner Area & Configurator (TechnikPRO)

**„Dla partnerów" (For partners)** — page `/dla-partnerow.html`. A resource hub for authorized dealers/business partners rather than a public sign-up program. It surfaces two things: **TechnikPRO** (links to manufacturer-specific dealer portals) and **Marketing** (link to `marketing.motowektor.pl`). # Part B — Realizacje (Portfolio), Sieć Serwisowa (Service Network), Instrukcje/Filmy

## 1. Realizacje / Gallery — `galeria.html`

**Section label:** "Realizacje" (Realizations/Projects). There is no large on-page headline; content begins directly with the project grid.

**How projects are showcased**
- **Layout:** A single continuous **thumbnail grid** — roughly **80+ entries**. Each tile pairs a photo with a short Polish title.
- **Linking:** Each thumbnail is **clickable and links to its own detailed sub-page**; inline descriptions are minimal, so detail lives on the per-project pages.
- **Captions:** Short, spec-oriented titles rather than marketing copy — e.g. **„Zabudowa 'szeroka' 2450mm"** (wide body, 2450 mm), **„Burtofirana jednostronna"** (single-sided curtain side), **„kabina sypialna"** (sleeping cabin).
- **Imagery style:** Professional exterior photography of finished vehicles shot from angles that show the bodywork build. Portfolio spans multiple years (~2008–present).

**Filters / categories:** **No filtering mechanism** — no tabs, dropdowns, or category buttons. Vehicle/work types emerge **implicitly from the titles**.

**Categories of work that appear (implicit, from titles):**
- **Cabs / sleeping quarters:** "Twin Cab L," "Twin Cab S," "kabina sypialna"
- **Containers / box bodies:** aluminium containers, "izoterma," honeycomb-panel containers, "Kontener"
- **Open beds / tippers:** "skrzynia otwarta," "wywrotka trójstronna"
- **Specialized builds:** "chłodnia," "foodtruck," "furgon warsztatowy," inspection vehicles
- **Curtainside:** "Burtofirana"
- **Base chassis used:** Renault Master, Iveco Daily, Fiat Ducato, Opel Movano, Mercedes Sprinter, Citroën Jumper, Peugeot

## 2. Service Network — `siec_serwisowa.html`

**Heading:** "Sieć serwisowa MOTO WEKTOR".

**Intro (verbatim):** „Mapa sieci serwisowej w której można wykonać przegląd okresowy zabudowy." (Map of the service network where you can carry out a periodic inspection of the bodywork.)

**How service points are presented**
- **Map:** A visual **map of the service network** is the central element, letting customers locate nearby points geographically.
- **Partner list:** References „listę koncesji z którymi współpracujemy" (the list of dealerships we cooperate with), reached via a dedicated link rather than a region-grouped on-page list. There is **no explicit region/voivodeship grouping** on the page itself.
- **Core service offered:** „przegląd okresowy zabudowy" (periodic inspection of the bodywork).
- **Contact / hours:** Sales Mon–Fri 8:00–16:00; service & spare parts Mon–Fri 7:00–15:00. Address: ul. Poligonowa 35, 43-300 Bielsko-Biała.

**External service-partner brands shown (logos linking to their dealer locators):** Dhollandia, Thermo King, Zanotti, Carrier, Webasto, Autoterm, Hwasung, Eberspächer.

**Value proposition:** The page is **thin on explicit value-proposition copy** — no dedicated paragraphs about warranty (gwarancja) or spare parts (części zamienne); the spare-parts mention is limited to opening hours. **RMI** appears only as a footer link → `https://motowektor.pl/web/rmi.pdf` with no surrounding text. (RMI = Repair and Maintenance Information.) The page functions mainly as a **map + partner-locator + periodic-inspection** hub.

## 3. Instrukcje / Filmy — `instrukcje-filmy.html`

**Section header:** "Do pobrania" (Downloads).

**Downloadable PDF manuals (~12 documents):**
- Heating/climate device operating manuals: **Webasto, Eberspächer, Autoterm, Thermo King**
- Equipment guides: **Hwasung** aggregates, **Viento, Dhollandia** systems
- **Zanotti** Z-series aggregate manual
- Pneumatic suspension instructions and **Drive-Rite** warranty documentation
- "Karta przeglądu okresowego" (periodic inspection checklist/card)
- In-cabin TV operation guide (**NVOX**)
- Two electrical wiring diagrams (**Renault** and **Opel**)

**Instructional videos (~24, linked to YouTube — not embedded inline):** dump-truck/tipper bodies (incl. new Master IV), sliding partition walls and refrigerated cargo units, pneumatic lifting systems (single- and dual-stage), tail-lift mechanisms (self-loading, column-type, slider-mounted), cab interiors (TwinCab, sleeping cabins), manual height-adjustment levers, retractable ramps and tarps, central post (stanchion) install/removal, air suspension systems.

**Format summary:** A mix of **downloadable third-party equipment PDFs + MotoWektor's own inspection checklist**, plus a library of **YouTube how-to videos** covering both operating installed equipment and the bodywork features MotoWektor builds.
