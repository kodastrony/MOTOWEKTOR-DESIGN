# MotoWektor — Brand & Visual Identity Research

Sources reviewed: `identyfikacja-wizualna.html` (visual identity), homepage `motowektor.pl/`, and `wsparcie-marketingowe.html` (marketing support). The dedicated visual-identity page carries almost all of the formal brand rules; the homepage and marketing page supply tone, imagery and downloadable materials.

## Logo

**Description & history:** The company rebranded its logo in **2016** — *„Z przyjemnością informujemy że zmieniliśmy logo naszej firmy"* (We are pleased to inform you that we have changed our company logo). The mark is a two‑colour (yellow + grey) "Moto Wektor" wordmark/symbol, supplied in both a **square (kwadrat)** lockup and a **horizontal (poziome)** lockup.

**Variants and their stated use cases (quoted verbatim):**
- Square logo — *„Do zastosowania na białym i kolorowym tle"* (for use on white and coloured backgrounds)
- Horizontal logo — *„Do zastosowania na białym i kolorowym tle"* (for use on white and coloured backgrounds)
- Light/white version — *„do zastosowania na fotografiach i ciemnych tłach"* (for use on photographs and dark backgrounds)
- Engraving, elongated surface — *„Grawer - powierzchnia podłużna"*
- Engraving, square surface — *„Grawer - powierzchnia kwadratowa"*
- Embroidery pattern — *„Wzór stosowany w hafcie na ciemnych materiałach"* (pattern used in embroidery on dark materials), supplied in light grey
- White-background version (for digital files needing maximum clarity)
- Banner version with transparency

## Brand Colors

The only **explicitly stated** brand colour spec is in Pantone (quoted verbatim): *„Żółty: PANTONE 137 C, szary: PANTONE 446 C"* (Yellow: PANTONE 137 C, Grey: PANTONE 446 C).

- **Primary / accent — Yellow:** PANTONE 137 C (a warm amber/golden yellow; approx. `#FFA400` / RGB ~255,164,0 as a reference equivalent — *not stated on the site*)
- **Secondary — Grey:** PANTONE 446 C (a dark, slightly warm charcoal grey; approx. `#3F4444` / RGB ~63,68,68 as a reference equivalent — *not stated on the site*)
- **Neutrals:** white/light backgrounds dominate the website, with dark grey/near‑black body text. The overall on-site palette reads as **yellow accent + grey + white/black**.

No HEX, RGB or CMYK values are published anywhere on the pages; the site does not state which element (lettering vs. background) takes which Pantone. The HEX/RGB approximations above are reference conversions only.

## Typography / Fonts

No font names or typographic rules are specified on any of the three pages. From the rendered site, the type is a **clean sans-serif** used hierarchically (larger sizes for navigation/headings, regular weight for body). Headings vs. body cannot be distinguished by named typeface — the site does not declare one.

## Photography & Imagery Style

- **Vehicle-centric, documentary/product style** rather than lifestyle. The homepage slider and "realizacje" (completed projects) show real commercial vehicles (e.g. Iveco Daily, Renault, Toyota) with MotoWektor bodies fitted.
- Vehicles are shown largely **in operational/on-location context**, not isolated on seamless white studio backgrounds.
- Imagery skews **practical and technical** — finished installations on actual trucks, plus catalog-page screenshots in the marketing section. People are not the focus; the product (the body/zabudowa) is.

## Taglines / Slogans / Key Messaging

- **Primary tagline:** *„Sprawdzone zabudowy samochodów dostawczych"* (Proven / tried-and-tested commercial (delivery) vehicle bodies).
- **Customisation message (repeated):** *„Każdy projekt dopasowujemy do potrzeb klienta"* (We tailor every project to the client's needs).
- **Marketing section heading:** *„Wsparcie marketingowe - katalogi i materiały do pobrania"* (Marketing support — catalogs and materials to download).
- **Rebrand announcement:** *„Z przyjemnością informujemy że zmieniliśmy logo naszej firmy"*.

Core product vocabulary that recurs as messaging: *zabudowy* (bodies), *skrzynie* (cargo/box bodies, incl. tarpaulin), *kontenery* (containers), *izotermy/chłodnie* (insulated/refrigerated), *furgony* (vans), *kabiny sypialne / TwinCab* (sleeper cabs).

## Tone of Voice

Professional, straightforward, technical-but-accessible, and service/solution-oriented. The copy emphasises **proven quality** and **client-specific customisation**. The visual-identity page adds a notably **controlled, compliance-minded** tone around brand usage.

## Stated Visual-Identity Rules / Brand Guidelines

- **Approval requirement (the only hard rule, quoted):** *„Każde użycie musi zostać zatwierdzone przez firmę"* (Every use must be approved by the company [Moto Wektor Sp. z o.o.]).
- **Format-by-use rules:** raster **PNG** files are for **A4 and smaller** applications; **vector (.cdr "curves")** files are required for **larger-format printing**.
- **Background-specific variants:** dedicated logo versions for white/coloured backgrounds, for dark backgrounds/photographs (light version), for engraving (two surface shapes), and for embroidery on dark materials.
- **Colour standard:** the two Pantone references (137 C / 446 C) act as the brand's colour spec.
- **No explicit "don'ts"** are published — there is no stated rule against recolouring, distorting, or otherwise modifying the logo beyond the blanket "must be approved by the company" requirement.
